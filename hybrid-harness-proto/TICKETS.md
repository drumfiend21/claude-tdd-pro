## Conventions

- Commit message format: `TICKET-NNN: <verb> <object>`
- One ticket per CL. If scope grows mid-ticket, split into TICKET-NNN.a / .b rather than expanding the original.
- Closing a ticket means the "Done when" column passes and is verified in the commit body.
